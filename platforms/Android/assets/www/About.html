About US

CTL Packaging is well known in Europe for its high end plastic extruded tube packaging. Our new venture into the USA is focused on a cutting edge innovation in tubes called the easysupplytube, the ultimate solution for the plastic industry. Easysupplytube is an injection molded plastic tube, with an in-mold label. This technology combines superior decoration, improved supply chain performance with optimal run sizes and reduced lead-times.

Mission:Position CTL Packaging USA, as the preferred choice for customers in the plastic tube market in North America that seek a high degree of service, superior decoration capabilities and are driven by value.
